Scratch Tic Tac Toe

Overview

With this project I aimed to create a version of tic tac toe withing scracth that can be played between two users on the same computer. It's not a very smart game and msot of the work is left to the users to do like deciding the winner, changing between X and O, etc.

Current Limitations and Possible Improvements

Of course this can't be the final version of this game. Obviously a system to detect when a straight or diagnol line of Xs or Os being formed would be required to call this a proper project. It would also be important to not have to click twice just to put Os and for the game to recognize turns. For the time being the project still needs more improvement.
