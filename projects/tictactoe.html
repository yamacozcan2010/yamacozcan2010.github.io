Scratch Tic Tac Toe

Overview

A Scratch Tic Tac Toe with: turn switching between X and O, win detection, victory backdrops, and a 5-second auto reset using broadcasts.

How it works

  • Global variables: X, O, Draw.
  • Stage controller watches scores in a forever loop.
  • Broadcasts HIDE_ALL and NEW_ROUND to hide/show all squares & UI.
  • Each square resets costume on NEW_ROUND.
